About the Team

Dragon Swim Team is a year-round competitive group focused on technique, endurance, and team spirit. From beginners to advanced athletes, we build better swimmers through developmental and competitive stages.

Training Groups

Age-group, junior, and senior lanes keep every swimmer challenged.

Meet Readiness

Weekly race sets and starts/turns work help swimmers stay prepared.

Team Culture

Respect, discipline, and support define who we are in and out of the pool.

About the Coaches

Our coaching staff combines competitive experience with athlete-first leadership. Each coach works closely with swimmers to improve stroke mechanics, race strategy, and confidence.

Head Coach Alex Rivera

Specializes in freestyle and IM development with data-driven training plans.

Assistant Coach Maya Chen

Leads stroke clinics and dryland sessions focused on injury prevention.

Assistant Coach Jordan Lee

Supports sprint groups with starts, underwater phases, and race execution.
